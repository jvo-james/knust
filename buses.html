KNUST School of Business

The KSB is the flagship business school on campus, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Management, Finance, and Marketing. Located at the heart of KNUST’s South Campus.

  • Accredited AACSB–level curriculum
  • Modern library & collaboration spaces
  • Café & student lounges

Medical Village

Home to the University Hospital and allied health centers. The Medical Village provides multi‑specialty care and clinical training facilities.

  • 500+ bed capacity
  • Teaching laboratories & research wing
  • 24/7 emergency services

Brunei

Brunei is the lush, green zone on campus known for its woodlands and quiet study spots. Perfect for evening strolls and outdoor lectures.

  • Nature trails & picnic areas
  • Outdoor Wi‑Fi hotspots
  • Open‑air classrooms

Commercial Area

The bustling marketplace with shops, eateries, and banks. A favorite spot for lunch breaks and weekend hangouts.

  • 20+ restaurants & cafes
  • Retail outlets & convenience stores
  • ATMs & banking services
